ABOUT ORREY

Orrey, the Tech Fest of Miranda House is a commemorative event to celebrate technology. Organised every year by the Computer Science department of Miranda House, the fest showcases, and acknowledges innovations and forthcoming opportunities in the field. Orrey 2017 will be, as per tradition, an exhibition of plethora of young talent and enthusiasm in technology along with being a festival filled with fun and excitement. The theme for Orrey 2017 is “Digitalization” in sync with the new cashless scheme by the Government of India. Demonetization was a move by the Government of India to curb black money, corruption and fake money menace. Since 8th November 2016, every Indian citizen has only one thing in mind: the dilemma of choosing a safe, secure, convenient and cashless payment option. Currently available cashless payment systems include credit/debit cards, e-wallets such as Paytm, Unified Payment Interface (UPI), IMPS, USSD, RTGS and NEFT. With this year’s technical fest, we aim to empower Digitalization and motivate others to say “No to Line...Pay Online”.
